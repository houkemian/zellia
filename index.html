Focus on what matters today

Zellia avoids complex dashboards and brings family health management back to today's tasks, vital sign records, and trackable family reminders.

Today's Medications

Automatically expands daily medication schedules into individual tasks, with check-ins, undo, soft stop, and long-term plans.

Vital Sign Logs

Quickly record blood pressure, heart rate, and glucose. Review history by page and notify family when readings need attention.

Family Voice Reminders

Family members can record a personal medication reminder voice, making reminders more natural and easier to notice.

Family Care

Build authorized family connections with invite codes or dynamic QR codes, so caregivers can view and help within approved permissions.

Weekly Summaries & Reports

Summarize medication adherence and vital trends, then export PDFs for family review or doctor visits.

Home Widget

Sync key medication and health information to a home screen widget, reducing the need to open the app.

A complete loop for family care

From reminders to action, from records to review, Zellia helps families ask fewer repetitive questions and coordinate with more clarity.

Set the Plan

Add medication plans for yourself or an authorized family member, with multiple daily times and long-term schedules.

Act on Time

Older adults check off tasks on the Today screen and can hear a family-recorded voice reminder when needed.

Record Vitals

Enter blood pressure, heart rate, and glucose with large controls that reduce complicated typing.

Review Together

Authorized caregivers review weekly summaries, history, and alerts to support long-term consistency.

Who It Is For

  • Older adults who take long-term medication or regularly track blood pressure and glucose.
  • Adult children and caregivers who want fewer check-in calls and clearer remote visibility.
  • Families who want reminders, records, and reports in one simple tool.

Design Principles

  • Reduce deep menus and prioritize today's tasks on the home screen.
  • Use larger buttons and tap targets with highly readable text.
  • Keep family data access permission-based, with checks for sensitive actions.
Zellia is a lifestyle and family health execution tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency response, or clinical monitoring. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions; in an emergency, contact local emergency services.
