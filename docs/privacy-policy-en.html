2. Who this Policy applies to

The Services are intended for individuals who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

3. Information we collect

We collect information in the following categories, depending on how you use Zellia.

3.1 Account and profile information

  • Nickname or display name you choose.
  • Identifiers used for third‑party sign‑in, when you choose to log in with a supported provider (for example, account identifiers or tokens provided by the authentication service). We do not receive your third‑party account password from those providers in a way that lets us access your messages or unrelated profile data beyond what is needed to authenticate you.

3.2 Health and wellness information you enter

Zellia is designed to help families organise information you voluntarily record, which may include, for example:

  • Blood pressure readings and related notes.
  • Medication records and reminders you set.
  • Other health or wellness‑related entries you choose to add in the app.

Important: You decide what to enter. Do not enter information you are not comfortable storing digitally.

3.3 Technical and operational data

We may collect limited technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the Services (for example, device type, app version, diagnostic logs, and security‑related events). This is described in more detail in our integration sections below.

3.4 Family linking and authorisation data

To enable family collaboration, we process information needed to create and manage authorised relationships between accounts (for example, activation codes, QR code scans, or similar flows you use to bind accounts). We use this to enforce only the sharing you explicitly authorise.

4. How we use your information

We use personal information only for purposes that are reasonably necessary to provide the Services, including to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Synchronise and display the information you record among family members you have authorised (for example, designated guardians or linked family accounts, as implemented in the app).
  • Generate health summaries or reports for you and your authorised family members, based on data you have entered.
  • Operate subscription features for Zellia PRO (see Section 7).
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the Services, detect abuse, and comply with law.

We do not sell your health information or personal information to third‑party advertisers or data brokers. We do not use your health records to serve third‑party behavioural advertising.

5. Legal bases (where applicable, e.g. EEA / UK)

Depending on your location, we may rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract with you (providing the Services you request).
  • Consent, where we ask for it (for example, for certain optional features or marketing communications, if offered).
  • Legitimate interests that are not overridden by your rights (for example, securing the Services and preventing fraud), where permitted by law.
  • Legal obligation, where we must comply with applicable law.

6. How family sharing works (and how to stop it)

  • Sharing happens only after you clearly authorise a family relationship (for example, by using an activation code or scanning a QR code to establish binding, as provided in the app).
  • You may withdraw access by unlinking or removing the family relationship in the app, in line with the features we provide. When you do so, the other party should no longer be able to access your information through that relationship going forward (subject to reasonable technical propagation time).

Family sharing is a core feature; if you do not want any sharing, you should not create or accept family links.
