Medical disclaimer Zellia is a lifestyle and wellness tracking tool, NOT a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. Do not rely on Zellia for emergency or clinical purposes.

1. Who we are

Zellia is operated by Hainan Huace Zhikang Huanbao Gongcheng Co., Ltd., located at Beijing, China. For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@dothings.one.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Service:

  • Account information: Your email address and authentication credentials when you register or sign in.
  • Wellness and vitals data: Health-related metrics you voluntarily enter, such as blood pressure, blood glucose, medication schedules, and reminder preferences.
  • Family voice reminders: Audio recordings you create using the microphone feature for personalized reminders. These files are stored on Cloudflare R2 object storage.
  • Device and usage data: Basic technical information (e.g., app version, device type, crash logs) to maintain security, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Subscription status: Information related to PRO subscriptions processed by Apple App Store or Google Play (we do not receive your full payment card details).

We do not knowingly collect sensitive categories beyond what you choose to enter for wellness tracking. You are responsible for the accuracy of data you submit.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide medication reminders, vitals logging, and family voice reminder features;
  • Authenticate your account and sync data across your devices;
  • Process and validate PRO subscription entitlements via Apple and Google;
  • Respond to support requests and send service-related communications;
  • Improve reliability, security, and user experience of the Service;
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. We do not sell your wellness or voice data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your vitals data, medication records, or voice recordings to third parties for advertising or data brokerage purposes. Your wellness entries and audio files are used solely to operate Zellia as described in this policy.

5. How we share information

We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help us host data (including Cloudflare R2 for voice storage), send emails, or operate infrastructure, under contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • App store platforms: Apple and Google, for subscription management and compliance with their policies.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protection consistent with this policy.

We do not share your vitals or voice recordings with third parties for their independent marketing use.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data, vitals entries, and voice files for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize associated personal data within a reasonable period, as described in our Data Deletion page.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit where supported and access controls on our systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; you use the Service at your own risk.
