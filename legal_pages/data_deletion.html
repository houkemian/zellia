Account & Data Deletion

Zellia · Last updated: [Last Updated Date]

This page explains how users of Zellia, operated by [Company Legal Name], can request deletion of their account and associated personal data. This process is provided in accordance with Google Play and Apple App Store data deletion requirements.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove or anonymize, where applicable:

  • Your registered email address and account profile;
  • Vitals and wellness entries you entered (e.g., blood pressure, blood glucose, medication logs);
  • Family voice reminder audio files stored on our servers (including Cloudflare R2);
  • App preferences and reminder configurations linked to your account.

Aggregated, de-identified analytics that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained for service improvement and legal compliance.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

Delete your account directly inside Zellia:

  1. Open the Zellia app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm when prompted.

This action is irreversible. Deletion typically completes within [Deletion Processing Time, e.g., 30 days], with most data removed sooner from active systems.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by contacting us. Send an email from the address associated with your Zellia account to:

[Support Email]

Use this subject line exactly:

Subject: Account Deletion Request Body: Please delete my Zellia account and all associated data. Registered email: [your email address] (Optional) Reason for deletion: [optional]

We may ask you to verify ownership of the email address before processing the request.

Active subscriptions

Deleting your Zellia account does not automatically cancel an Apple or Google PRO subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription in your App Store or Google Play account settings before or after account deletion. See our Subscription Terms in the Terms of Service.

Processing timeline

  • In-app deletion: Initiated immediately; backup copies may persist for up to [Backup Retention Period, e.g., 30 days] before permanent erasure.
  • Email requests: We aim to confirm receipt within [Response SLA, e.g., 7 business days] and complete deletion within [Completion SLA, e.g., 30 days].

Data we may retain

We may retain limited information only when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, tax, or fraud-prevention requirements;
  • Resolve disputes or enforce our agreements;
  • Maintain security logs for a defined retention period.

Any retained data is restricted and not used for marketing.
